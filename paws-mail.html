The Idea

I don't know where this idea came from, but the moment I had it I was already building. It's a digital pet that lives on my dock — I can pick from a cat, a dog, a pigeon, a fox, or a rabbit, each in a few colors — and when I send someone a message, my pet walks off my screen and into theirs to deliver it, and they can send their pet back to bring me a letter too. I forced the people I love to install it so our pets could visit each other over the same network. Mine's a pigeon, obviously. It's imperfect, a little crafty, and yes — we could just text. But seeing their pet wander into my screen every now and then just feels right.
